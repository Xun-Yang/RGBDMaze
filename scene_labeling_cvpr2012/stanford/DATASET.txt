The Stanford Background Dataset is from Gould et al and available at:

http://dags.stanford.edu/projects/scenedataset.html

Run the script convert_dataset.m to download the dataset and rename the files
with numerical names.

You also need gPb-ucm segmentations of the images, available here:

http://homes.cs.washington.edu/~xren/research/cvpr2012_scene/stanford.zip
